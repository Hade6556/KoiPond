Frequently Asked Questions 🤔

Everything founders ask before their first pitch with Koi Pond

🎯 How does Koi Pond actually help me pitch? +

You upload your deck and pick a scenario — pre-seed, seed, Demo Day, accelerator interview. Koi Pond plays a skeptical investor who probes your wedge, market, traction, and ask. Afterward you get a scored breakdown and the exact moments the pitch wobbled.

💼 What fundraising stages does Koi Pond support? +

From friends & family through Series A. Each investor persona is tuned to what partners at that stage actually push on — vision at pre-seed, traction at seed, unit economics at Series A.

💻 Does Koi Pond work in my real investor calls? +

Yes — Koi Pond runs alongside Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. Live coaching cards surface in a side panel only you can see, so the investor never knows it's there.

🔒 What happens to my deck and pitch data? +

Your deck and recordings are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never train models on your pitches, and you can delete everything in one click. Founders own their data — full stop.

⚡ How quickly can I run my first practice pitch? +

Under two minutes. Sign up, upload your deck or paste your one-pager, pick an investor persona, and you're pitching.

📦 Do I need to install anything? +

Nope — Koi Pond runs in your browser. An optional desktop helper is available if you want richer audio capture during real calls.

👥 Can my co-founder practice alongside me? +

Yes. Invite your co-founder to a shared workspace, run two-on-one pitches, and review each other's breakdowns. Team plans add shared playbooks and pitch history.
