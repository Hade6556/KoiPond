Frequently Asked Questions 🤔

What people ask before their first run with Koi Pond

🎯 How does Koi Pond actually help me? +

Pick the speaking moment you're dreading — interview, class presentation, toast, conference talk. Koi Pond plays the audience for that moment and asks the questions a real room would ask. Afterward you get a scored breakdown on clarity, pacing, confidence, and filler words — plus the exact second your delivery wobbled.

💼 What kinds of speaking does Koi Pond cover? +

Job interviews, class presentations, wedding toasts, conference talks, networking intros, hostile Q&A, podcast appearances, sales calls, eulogies — every speaking moment that scares people. Each scenario has its own audience temperament: warm, neutral, skeptical, hostile, busy.

💻 Do I need anyone else around to practice? +

Nope — that's the point. You practice alone, in your room, with just your camera and microphone. Koi Pond is the audience, the interviewer, and the coach. Nobody else has to see your shaky first attempts.

🔒 What happens to my recordings? +

Your recordings stay private — encrypted in transit and at rest. We never train models on your practice runs, and you can delete everything in one click. Your nervous early attempts are yours alone.

⚡ How quickly can I start? +

Under two minutes. Click into the dashboard, pick a scenario, grant mic + camera, and start talking. No upload required, no signup blocker.

📦 Do I need to install anything? +

No — Koi Pond runs entirely in your browser. Just mic and camera permissions. Works on Mac, Windows, and most newer iPad/Android setups.

💸 What's free vs. paid? +

Free: one practice run per day, full scorecard, four core scenarios (job interview, class presentation, networking intro, conference talk). Pro at $19/mo: unlimited runs, every scenario unlocked, longer runs (up to 10 min), deeper analytics, filler-word and pacing drills. Cancel anytime, 7-day refund.
