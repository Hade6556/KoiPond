Methodology

How ranks are calculated · anti-gaming rules

Anti-gaming rules

  • • Only one row per (user, scenario) — your single best score.
  • • Pitch must be ≥ 30 seconds and at least 2 of 3 Q&A turns answered.
  • • Re-submitting the same transcript word-for-word is silently dropped.
  • • Opt-in is per-user; non-opted entries are invisible to everyone but the owner.

Career Score formula

  • • weighted_total = Σ (best_score × difficulty_multiplier)
  • • Multipliers: easy ×1.00 · medium ×1.15 · hard ×1.30 · challenge ×1.45
  • • career_score = (weighted_total / scenarios_attempted) × (1 + log₁₀(scenarios) × 0.15)
  • • Rewards both quality (avg) and breadth (log-bonus per scenario tried).
